The first step to improving performance is measurement. EngeniusMicro develops custom sensing solution platforms for industrial and military customers. We specialize in low power, high value unobtrustive sensing solutions. Let us leverage our collection of Sensing solutions to meet your needs.

Our Sensing Toolbox


  • Power

    If your solution can be powered by mains, rechargable or replaceable batteries or demands more exotic power solutions like vibration or RF energy harvesting we have the experience to develop a sysmte to meet your needs.

  • Environment

    We have developed sensor systems for standard commerical and military environments and more demanding environments including corrosive chemcials, nuclear storage casks, and extreme temperatures. Our sensors can be attached or embedded in many structural systems.
